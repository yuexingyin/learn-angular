About Kiditech

In Kiditech's mighty world, we coach, play and celebrate an innovative technology program: K-12 STEAM. We gather at Kiditech to learn and have fun with Robots Design, 3D Design and Print, IoT with Arduino and Raspberry Pi, Game Design and more. Together, we discover the evolution of technology and have fun with science, engineering, art and math!

Why choose us

  • Largest selection of high-tech courses in the Bay Area
  • Progressive course levels for children of different levels
  • Project-based courses backed with a well-defined curriculum
  • Great benefits with the low student-to-teacher ratio
  • Highly qualified instructors with tech and engineering background
  • Scholarships available for underprivileged and top performing students
