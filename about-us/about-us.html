About Kiditech

Kiditech is an innovative educational 501(c)(3) non-profit organization focusing on providing technology enrichment for K-12 students.

In 2016, bay area engineer parents from tech giants like Amazon, Apple, Visa, Linkedin, Broadcom etc. founded Kiditech.org over the garage, we are fan of doing cool things like DIY Robots, 3D design and printing, coding, designing a 3D cool game.

As parents, we believe the experience of learning technology is fun,Immersed, and intuitive. Kids will gain engineering and coding skill, logical thinking, teamwork, confidence, patience, persistence through project-based fun projects.

Kiditech believe equal eduation, and would provide scholarship for underprivileged Students, please refer to scholarship application page for detail.

Why choose us

Kiditech provides mulitiple-level technology immersed curriculum to global K-12 students, including Robots (from Wonder dash to DIY Robots), Drone design, 3D design and Printing, IOT with open source hardware, Game design (from Stratch to 3D AR/VR game), Artificial intelligence etc.
